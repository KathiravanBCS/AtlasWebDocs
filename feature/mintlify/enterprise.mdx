For larger orgs, the decisions are organizational, not just technical.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Access control & SSO" icon="lock">
    Gate private docs behind SSO and role-based access. Keep public and internal
    content cleanly separated — ideally distinct deployments — so an
    authorization mistake can't leak internal pages.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Monorepo vs. multi-repo" icon="folder-tree">
    A **monorepo** keeps docs next to code (easy to keep in sync, one review flow).
    **Multi-repo** suits independent teams but needs a strategy to assemble a unified
    portal. Choose based on team boundaries, not aesthetics.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Multi-product portals" icon="grid-2">
    Use top-level **dropdowns or tabs** to switch products while sharing branding and
    search. Resist duplicating shared concepts across products — factor them into
    snippets.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Audit & compliance" icon="clipboard-check">
    Your Git history is already an audit log for content. Pair it with platform-level
    audit logging for access and configuration changes to satisfy compliance reviews.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
