## Make content AI-retrievable

The same habits that help humans help retrieval:

- **One clear job per page** — narrow pages chunk and retrieve better than sprawling ones.
- **Descriptive `title` + `description`** — these become the retrieval signal.
- **Real headings, not bold text** — structure is what chunking keys off.
- **Self-contained sections** — assume a reader (or model) landed mid-page from search.

<Tip>
  You usually don't author `llms.txt` by hand — Mintlify generates it. Your job is
  to write clean, well-structured Markdown so the generated artifact is high quality.
</Tip>
